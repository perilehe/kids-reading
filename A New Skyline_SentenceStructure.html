Introduction

Iconic structures such as these, along with surrounding buildings, create an outline against the sky, called a skyline.

Sentence Core (主干)

structures + create + an outline

structures (S) create (V) an outline (O)

Everything else just adds more information!

Vocabulary Quiz

1. "Iconic" in this sentence is closest in meaning to:
"Iconic structures such as these..."
famous and recognizable
old and historical
tall and impressive
beautiful and colorful
✓ "Iconic" means something is very famous and everyone recognizes it as a symbol!
